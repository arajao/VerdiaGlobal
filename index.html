About Verdia Global

Verdia Global is a Madagascar-based exporter specializing in raw sisal fiber. We operate close to the source in southern Madagascar, ensuring consistent sorting, dry storage, and export-ready packing for international buyers.

Our focus is reliability — predictable grades, stable volumes, and professional export coordination for long-term supply relationships.

Sisal Fiber Grades

UG – Long Fiber (70cm+)

High-quality long fiber suitable for industrial and reinforcement applications. Carefully sorted and packed in export bales.

Short UG (<70cm)

Shorter fiber grade with consistent availability and stable supply.

Capacity & Logistics

Monthly capacity up to two 40ft containers. Shipments via Toalagnaro (Fort Dauphin), Madagascar.

  • Incoterms: FOB / CIF
  • Container-ready bales
  • Pre-loading inspection photos available

Quality Approach

We apply visual inspection during sorting, maintain dry storage before loading, and coordinate shipments carefully to maintain consistency and grade separation.
